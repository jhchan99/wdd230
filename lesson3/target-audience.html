Target Audience

My target audience are any new immagrant Chinese Americans who are new to their city or town.

Personas

Jennifer just moved to her new town 1 week ago. She's all settled in to her new home but school doesnt start for her kids for another month! She needs places to visit that are friendly and familiar. She also wants to know if there are stores nearby that sell things she needs to make chinese dishes.


David has been in America for two weeks and has been working since he got here. He wants to go do something fun this weekend and wants to meet some new people. He's also thinking about starting his own restaraunt in the next year and wants to know if there is a market for that in his city.
